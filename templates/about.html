About Caden Traders

About Us

Caden Traders is a partnership firm founded by Allen Roy and Daniel Roy, driven by a shared passion for financial markets and a commitment to long-term wealth maximisation. Specializing in trading and investment, the firm operates with a clear focus on generating stable income and building sustainable financial growth.

At the heart of Caden Traders lies a vision rooted in systematic and disciplined trading. The firm leverages structured strategies across intraday, swing, and positional trading, guided by deep market analysis and prudent risk management.

With a purpose-driven approach, Caden Traders is dedicated to empowering capital through consistency, clarity, and control — turning market opportunities into lasting value.

Vision Statement

"To build a high-performance trading enterprise through structured strategies, disciplined execution, and long-term value creation."

Mission Statement

“To generate stable income and achieve sustainable wealth creation through a disciplined and risk-managed approach”
